Elmwood-Murdock Public Schools is a K-12 district located in Murdock (High School) and Elmwood (Elementary), Nebraska. The elementary school is located at 400 West F in Elmwood Nebraska. The junior/senior high is located at 300 Wyoming in Murdock, Nebraska. To contact the high school in Murdock, please call (402) 867-2341, fax us at (402) 867-2009, or complete the Contact Form on the right side of this page.
