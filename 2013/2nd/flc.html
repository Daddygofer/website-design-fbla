Fall Leadership Conference (FLC)

Attention new members! This one-day event for the West Central Region occurring at Cedarcrest High School on October 19 offers various workshops, classes and diverse events that FBLA members can participate in. It's mandatory that new members attend. However, returning members have the option of whether they would like to go or not. International Community School members who experienced success last year at the National Leadership Conference will be giving a presentation on "Steps to Success: Placing in FBLA Events." Be sure to check them out along with the other marvelous workshops! Please be at school before 7:30; we must leave precisely at 7:30. Directions can be found below and forms are in the Resources section.

October 19, 2012 | 7:30 am - 1:30 pm

Cedarcrest High School

29000 NE 150th St Duvall, WA 98019
