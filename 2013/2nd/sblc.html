State Business Leadership Conference (SBLC)

The final step before reaching the ultimate goal of the National Leadership Conference! Having already placed in the top five for our region, your proficiency for your specific event is already outstanding. However, keep in mind that you will be competing against the top five winners from each region. On average, performance events have anywhere between 30 and 60 groups competing, and only the top two groups advance to the National Leadership Conference. The State Business Leadership Conference takes place of the course of three days. In location switches between Spokane and Seattle, and this year it's in Spokane again. Though it's likely that you'll have a lot of down time between events, it's strongly encouraged that you participate in the workshops and other activities offered by business professionals.
