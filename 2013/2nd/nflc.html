National Fall Leadership Conference (NFLC)

Beginning in 2012, FBLA-PBL will be hosting a National Fall Leadership Conference in three separate locations, beginning in Indianapolis, IN on November 2nd. The following week Denver, CO will be hosting the second conference, and the third and final conference will be held in Charlotte, NC on November 16. This two-day event resembles local Fall Leadership Conferences while offering many of the advantages available at the National Leadership Conference in the Summer. During the conference members can attend workshops and professional development sessions, explore networking opportunities, and participate in many other activities. This conference does not require any prerequisites to qualify for attendance. A chapter must express enough interest in order for students to attend the conference in Denver, CO. If you would like to go, please email our chapter email: icsfbla@hotmail.com with your name and grade. More information regarding this situation will be presented in our weekly meetings once we have gathered sufficient feedback from members. Stay tuned for more information!
