Welcome to the Community Calendar!

This Community Calendar is a resource that displays information about previous and upcoming chapter and community events in a centralized place. Tabs at the top of the page organize events that are listed on the calendar into specific groups based on the type of event, for easier access.


Mission Statement: Our goal is to strengthen community ties by providing easy access to information to local activities taking place in the greater Seattle area. By increasing interaction between people sharing similar interests, whether it is in music taste, sports, or newfound hobbies, our community as a whole can prosper as relationships build and common goals are met.
