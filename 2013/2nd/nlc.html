National Leadership Conference (NLC)

The last stop of the year, the very thing that we have worked for: the National Leadership Conference offers an indescribable experience for the aspiring businessperson. This one week journey includes a plethora of networking opportunities, life-changing workshops, and of course the highest level of competitive events in FBLA-PBL. For the 2012-2013 FBLA-PBL year, the National Leadership Conference will take place in Anaheim, CA. Eligibility for this conference requires the prerequisite of placing first or second in a presentation-based competitive event or placing first, second or third in a test-based event at the State Business Leadership Conference. If you did not receive the aforementioned accolades, but still placed in the top five, then contact our adviser as he will receive notices regarding withdrawals from other competitors that could leave vacancies.
