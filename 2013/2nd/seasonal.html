St. Patrick's Day Parade

Every year, thousands of people gather in the streets of Seattle and partake in the St. Patrick's Day Parade, celebrating the Irish heritage in Seattle. The streets are filled with people crowing to see small floats and children parading down the road, waving to the audience like royalty. After the wonderful parade, the day ends with closing ceremonies at the famous Seattle Center (home of the Space Needle)!

Who:Anyone!
When:Saturday, March 16, 2013, 12:30 pm
Where:Starting from 4th Ave at Jefferson in downtown Seattle, traveling north on 4th Ave S and officially ending at the Seattle Center

Cirque du Soleil

Starting in 2013, Cirque du Soleil comes back to Redmond, WA after two years, bringing with it a brand new show called AMALUNA, a love story between the Princess of a wealthy land and a young suitor. Cirque du Soleil comes go Redmond every two years, each time somehow exceeding the previous show in terms of glamour and style. This event is fun for all ages as well, allowing for a great time among family. Make sure to reserve tickets as soon as possible because they are flying off the shelves as the season winds down.

Who:Anyone!
When:January 31, 2013 to March 24, 2013
Where:Marymoor Park (6046 W Lake Sammamish Parkway NE, Redmond, WA 98052)
