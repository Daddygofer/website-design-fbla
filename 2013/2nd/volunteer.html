As teenagers, many of us take for granted the luxuries that we possess. It's our duty to give back to the community what they have given us. In Kirkland alone, there are countless possibilities for working in the community to support various causes and programs. Our FBLA chapter provides members with the opportunity to assist several local organizations: Eastside Baby Corner, Park Cleanup and volunteering at the local Tent City. To learn more about our programs with the aforementioned organizations, simply click on the links below, or scroll over the 'Volunteering' tab and select a page.
