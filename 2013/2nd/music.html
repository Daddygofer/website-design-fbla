Concerts at Marymoor

Come to Marymoor Park this summer to enjoy the park's annual summer concert series. The state-of-the-art venue located in Willowmoor Farm region of Marymoor Park can accommodate over 5,000 people. Escape the summer heat and join us in the evening for some exuberant music!

Victoria Justice

"Here's 2 Us" Summer Tour

July 3, 2013 | 5:00pm

Click here for tickets!

Best known for her parts Nickelodeon productions "Zoey 101" and "Victorious," the teen actress has recently turned towards the music industry. Already the artist of several chart-topping hits, Justice looks forward to releasing her debut album in 2013. As part of her "Here's 2 Us Summer Tour," Justice will be performing on Wednesday, July 3.

Pink Martini

Pink Martini Summer Tour ft. China Forbes

August 4, 2013 | 6:00pm

Click here for tickets!

Founded in 1994, this musical group populates a specific niche in modern music. Their unique, orchestral sound crosses multiple genres, including: pop, classical, Latin and jazz. Pink Martini songs have been used in films such as Mr. & Mrs. Smith and television series such as The Sopranos and The West Wing. This summer, Pink Martini will be at Marymoor Park for one night only. Don't miss it!
