Park Cleanup

Our community provides many opportunities for service. With environment treatment receiving much publicity, it's more crucial now than ever to help preserve the indigenous aspects of nature that remain. The hundreds of parks on the greater Eastside are instrumental to the community. To help protect the beautiful landscapes and havens, the city youth boards of both Redmond and Sammamish are hosting park cleanup days.

To join in this cause for a cleaner, healthier environment, call event coordinator Lydia Wright at (425) 297-3548 or by email at waparksrec.community@outlook.com
