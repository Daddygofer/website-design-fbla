Outdoor Movies at Marymoor Park - Upcoming: Grease

Address: 6046 West Lake Sammamish Pkwy NE, Redmond, WA 98052

Phone: (206) 205-3661

Sponsored by First Tech Federal Credit Union, outdoor movies will be shown at Marymoor Park throughout the summer. Teens are encouraged to attend for appropriate features. Event features include food trucks, live acts, and an assortment of games regarding various movies. All flicks will be shown on a giant outdoor movie screen. Come every Friday beginning July 12th through August 30th. For the comprehensive list of movies, visit their event page.

Redmond Derby Days


Redmond City Hall Campus

15670 NE 85th St.
Redmond, WA 98052
(425) 556-2900

July 13-15, 2013

Originating in 1941, Redmond Derby Days has become an iconic Summer event on the Eastside. Suited for all ages, this 3-day event consists of an abundance of "fair-like" food, blowup activities for children, innovative booths run by representatives of companies, bike races, and parades just to name a few events. Thousands of people come to Downtown Redmond every Summer to take part in this incredible tradition. Come as a guest or call to sign up as a volunteer.
