Winter Leadership Conference (WLC)

On February, 7, 2013, hundreds of the West-Central Region's best will gather at the Meydenbauer Center, representing their respective schools and competing in events for the first time this year. The road to the National Leadership Conference begins here as placing in the top five in your respective event guarantees you a trip to the State Business Leadership Conference. This conference takes place over the course of one day, so no workshops or other other activities are offered.

February 7, 2013 | 7:30 am - 1:30 pm

Meydenbauer Center

11100 NE 6th St Bellevue, WA 98004

(425) 637-1020
