Welcome to the Polk County High School Youth Connection! Want to get involved in the community or at school? Many service-oriented school clubs, including FBLA, FCA, FFA, HOSA, NHS, SkillsUSA, and TSA, are described here along with activities the clubs participate in. There are also recent photos relating to each club and a promotional video showing what each is all about. A list of upcoming events including all of the various clubs' events is on the home page, and each club's specific activities are listed on its individual page. Feel free to contact us, view our copyright information, check out the local government's website to learn more ways to participate, or utilize our Mobile Site by using the links at the bottom. Also, find out more about this summer's FBLA national conference through the link on the right and connect with us and other youth through our social network pages by using the Facebook, Twitter and Google+ icons!
