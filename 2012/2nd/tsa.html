Technology Student Association

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is the only student organization devoted exclusively to the needs of students interested in technology. Open to students enrolled in or who have completed technology education courses, TSAâ€™s membership includes over 150,000 middle and high school students in 2,000 schools spanning 48 states.

Here in Polk County, Tennessee we are proud to represent our area on a regional, state and national level as we have done before. Our chapter is currently composed of a total of 160 members- a huge number compared to our student body of only 591. TSA is supported by educators, parents and business leaders who believe in the need for a technologically literate society. Members learn through exciting competitive events, leadership opportunities and much more. The diversity of activities makes TSA a positive experience for every student. From engineers to business managers, our alumni credit TSA with a positive influence on their lives. To become a member of our chapter, just bring $15 (for membership dues) and a competitive spirit!
