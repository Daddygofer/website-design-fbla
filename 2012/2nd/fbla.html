Future Business Leaders of America

FBLA Future Business Leaders of America-Phi Beta Lambda is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) education association with a quarter million students preparing for careers in business and business-related fields.

In our FBLA chapter, we have a total of 52 members, and are always accepting more! To join our chapter, you must pay membership dues of $12. In FBLA, we learn leadership and teamwork, use the newest software, practice our speeches, and perfect our professional dress. All of this is a precursor to our regional, state, and national competitions held from the spring to the middle of summer. Joining you in these conferences will be your officers and your advisors, Dewey Esquinance, Le-An Thomason, and Jason Adams.
