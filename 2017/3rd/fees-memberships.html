RESIDENT MEMBERSHIPS


Memberships are available for 3 month, 6 month and annual terms. The annual membership has the option to pay in full, or in monthly payments*. The monthly payment option is available to pay with a debit or credit card, and includes bank fees. Proof of current residency must be shown to qualify for the resident rates. A current bill with your name and address is preferred. A driver's license will not be accepted.
The *Family Membership* includes up to six family members.
The *Monthly Payments* option is only available by committing to the Annual Membership.

SENIOR PASS

SENIOR PASS


The Senior Pass gives members access to the Community side of the Recreation Center. This pass gives free access to core programs including - Tai Chi, Bingo, Zumba Gold, Book Club, Educational programs and seminars, limited track and swim access, Computer Lab, Library access and Wellness Room Fitness Equipment access. This pass does not give general access to the rest of the Recreation Center.
