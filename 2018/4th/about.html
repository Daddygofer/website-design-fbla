Gamification is a process in which elements typically found in a game are brought to the design world in order to engage the audience. 

Bring this concept to the tedious process of preparing for a job, and voila! You have LevelUp, a company aiming to assist younger peoples to enter the professional world in a comfortable and familiar environment. After all, the transition to the professional world can be a stressful and intimidating one, and we hope to provide some relief for any anxious job seekers.

As our users progress through the levels, they will be greeted by instructional pages that contain common lingo accompanied by visually minimalistic pages in order to not overwhelm anyone with information. When they have finished all of the levels and become a "LevelUp Master", they are officially ready to begin searching for a job. In Level Four, we provide ways in which users can take their learned skills and apply them to real life. 

DISCLAIMER: This is a fictitious site created for the FBLA Web Design competition. Tie tying demonstrations were taken from the Youtube Channel Ties.com. This website was developed using Pingendo, an IDE that allows us to easily incorporate HTML and CSS under the Bootstrap 4 framework. Several programming concepts were learned and implemented from W3Schools. Job-related information was taken from ResumeGenius, TheBalance, and LiveCareer. The suggested dress was inspired off of the FBLA dress code.
