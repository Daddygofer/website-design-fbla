About us

Gluteus Minimus has been a warm and family owned business for 5 years. We are all about making our guests satisfied and welcome. We are more than a restaurant. We are friends. We are family. We are Gluteus Minimus.

Here at Gluteus Minimus we serve the best breads, sandwiches, soups, and desserts. We have the finest ingredients and the best chefs. Ever since we opened we have focused on being gluten and GMO free. There just wasn't enough choices out there for good gluten free meals. We've decided to change that.

Make the choice and join us to live Gluten and GMO free. We plan on seeing you here soon.
